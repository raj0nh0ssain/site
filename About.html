Eastern University

Eastern University is a private university located in Dhanmondi, Dhaka, Bangladesh. The Eastern University was established in 2003 under the Private University Act 1992, and later on approved under Private University Act of 2010. The university was set up by Eastern University Foundation - a non-profit, non-political and philanthropic organization. Its founders include academics, chartered accountants, engineers, industrialists and retired civil servants. The Foundation has 30 members. The governance of Eastern University is carried out as per the Private Universities Act of 2010 by several bodies: Board of Trustees, Syndicate, Academic Council, Curriculum Committee, Finance Committee, Teacher Selection Committee and Disciplinary Committee.

He also hoped that Eastern University will play significant role in realizing the ‘Vision 2021’ and ‘Vision 2041’ of the Government of Bangladesh, thorough imparting quality education to its students.

Fees and Financial Assistance

In pursuance of the commitment of the Founders of the University to provide “higher education at affordable cost,” and to open opportunities of education to meritorious students of limited income, the tuition and other fees have been set at a reasonable level. In addition, several types of financial assistance, scholarships and a Student Loan scheme have been provided for. Details of the fees and financial assistance are available on the website of the university (www.easternuni.edu.bd)

Faculties and Programs

The University has at present four faculties:

  • Faculty of Arts
  • Faculty of Engineering & Technology
  • Faculty of Business Administration
  • Faculty of Law.

Information

Eastern University is recognized as one of the leading private universities in Bangladesh, much known for its quality educations in its chosen fields of education with a motto to become one of the best universities in South Asia. The University attracts talented and experienced faculty members for imparting quality education to its students at an affordable cost with a view to prepare them succeed in their careers and contribute significantly to nation building.

International Collaboration

Eastern University has collaborations with more than 20 internationally reputed institutions spanning the globe

Collaborations

The International Office of Eastern University has collaborations with more than 20 internationally reputed institutions spanning the globe including those in the US, UK, Europe, Southeast Asia, and Australasia. More than 24 international scholarships are available for students of Eastern University every year. The Yunnan Open University of China offers study tour and exchange program to the students of Eastern University. More than 50 students have visited and experienced the Chinese Language and Culture in the last three years. Following is a list of such universities and institutions:

  • University of Worcester. UK
  • Franklin University, Ohio, USA
  • Universiti Malaysia Perlis (UniMAP), Malaysia
  • Tampere University of Applied Science, Finland
  • Yunnan Normal University, China (YNNU)
  • University Utara Malaysia
  • Auckland Institute of Studies (AIS), St Helens, Auckland, New Zealand
  • University of Salford, Manchester
  • Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM)(YNNU)
